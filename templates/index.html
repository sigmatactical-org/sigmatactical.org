Own your vehicle’s software

A vehicle should not become a black box that stops evolving when a vendor moves on. Sigma Tactical Group publishes the libraries, firmware, and services behind Sigma Racer so owners, builders, and integrators can inspect, extend, and keep their machines running on their own terms.

Everything below lives in @{{ github_org|safe }}. Clone it, fork it, run it in your garage or on your cluster.

Why we open-source

Modern vehicles are computers on wheels. When the stack is closed, you inherit someone else’s roadmap, their end-of-life date, and their idea of what you are allowed to change. Open code flips that: calibration, instrumentation, commerce, and operations become yours to study, improve, and operate.

Use the sections below to find what matters for your work—vehicle firmware, bench tooling, commerce services, shared platform code, and public sites.
